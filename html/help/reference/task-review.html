<&| /help/_elements/wrapper, title => 'Task Review' &> Task review is a little something we cooked up to make it easier to look at everything you've put in Hiveminder. By doing all the legwork of stepping you through your tasks, we hope to strip as much monotony and pain from addressing your to do lists as possible. Once you agree to strap yourself in for a review, you have the following choices:
  • Take Action
    • Do this today
      This will set a "hide until" date for today, ensuring that the task will show up on any current search, starting today. It will also save any changes you make to the owner, the tags, or any other data on the screen.
    • Done!
      You're done! Enjoy the brain-applied seratonin hit as you click this. It's the equivalent of clicking the checkbox in your To Do list.
    • Next
      If you've changed any details (owner, tags, etc.) on the task, clicking "Next" will save the changes you've made and take you to the next task.
  • Ignore until...
    • Tomorrow
      Hides your task until tomorrow.
    • Saturday
      Hides your task until the next Saturday in the calendar. If it is Saturday when you click this, it moves to the next Saturday in the calendar.
    • Monday
      Hides your task until the next Monday in the calendar. If it is Monday when you click this, it moves to the next Monday in the calendar.
    • next month
      Hides your task until the same numeric date in the following month. For example, if you are reviewing on the 23rd of June, your task will hide until the 23rd of July.
  • Other...
    • Delete
      Removes the task forever. There's no getting it back! It does first ask you if you're sure, though.
    • Stop review
      Brings the task review to a screeching halt, and pops you back to where you were when you started it.
