<&| /help/_elements/wrapper, title => 'Default sort' &> The "Default" sort will order your tasks like so:
  1. By whether or not they're complete
  2. By due date
  3. By priority
  4. in order of creation.
It will only show the tasks you're searching for, however. For instance, it will not show your "done" tasks unless you have checked the box in "Search" to show "Done" tasks.
