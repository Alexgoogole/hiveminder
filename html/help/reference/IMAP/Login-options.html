If you're using a different client, the hostname to connect to is hiveminder.com Your username and password are your Hiveminder email address and password -- yes, this means your username will have an @ in it! You should configure your email client to connect using SSL or TLS encryption. To protect your security, Hiveminder's IMAP does not allow logins unless your client's connection is encrypted using SSL or TLS.
