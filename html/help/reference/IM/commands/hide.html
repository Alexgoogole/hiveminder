Lets you change the starting date of one or more tasks.

If you want to unset a starting date, use unhide tasks (which is really just "hide tasks until yesterday").

See also help context if you're wondering how the last example works!

Examples:

  • hide #5AP until friday
  • hide 5AP RO24 until yesterday
  • hide SAP for 5 days
  • hide 5AP forever
  • hide these until next week
