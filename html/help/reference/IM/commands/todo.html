<&| /help/_elements/wrapper, title => 'IM Command: Todo' &> %# terse: todo. Shows you your regular task list. http://tinyurl.com/2xhnnq

Shows you tasks you own that you haven't finished yet. This is the same list as your Hiveminder home page.

You can also use todo search terms to narrow down your todo list; this is useful if you're looking for a specific task or set of tasks to do.

It currently only supports your own tasks, not those of any of your groups.

Examples:

  • todo
  • todo meetup
  • t dinner
