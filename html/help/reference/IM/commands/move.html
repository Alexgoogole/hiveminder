<&| /help/_elements/wrapper, title => 'IM Command: Move' &> %# terse: move TASKS into GROUP. Changes the group of your tasks. http://tinyurl.com/yp5vch

Lets you change the group of one or more tasks.

Groups let many people easily collaborate on tasks. If you're working with group tasks, filter group NAME may also be of use to you.

See also help context if you're wondering how the last example works!

Examples:

  • move #TWD into SPECTRE
  • move #9AN to personal
  • move these into pretty good grocery
